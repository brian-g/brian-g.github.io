Here is a picture of our PT Cruiser. First bit of business, what is "PT Punch"? PT Punch is not a color or anything else having to do with an attribute of our car. "PT Punch" is a game that my wife and I play similar to "Slug Bug" where when we see another PT Cruiser, we the first to see it yells "PT Punch" and punches the other in the arm or leg. Juvenile? Yes it is, but it is our game.


Our Cruiser is a 2002 Deep Cranberry Touring Edition. It has a manual transmission (you aren't drivin' if you ain't shifting), cruise control and anti-lock brakes. We haven't done any real customizations yet. I would love to get a billet grill. A different grill would really spiff it up.
